I have gained a new and exciting skill set that will propel me into the next phase of my career as a value-creator

PROJECTS

Bus-boy was my first front end web app. Busboy is game about a first day hire at an Italian restaurant. For his first task he is told to bust the tables, and the first order of business is to get those dirty tablecloths off the table. Theres only one problem, the tables are coming so fast the theres almost no time to collect the sheets. Time for a little old fashioned cloth pulling, but be careful, pull too quick or too slow and the dirty dishes will fly right on to the floor

Memer is an app created using MEN stack (MongoDb, Mongoose, Express, and NodeJS). It's purpose was simple: Make an application that can take some of my all time favorite memes and make them into my own! Soon to come is the ability to upload photos, and export photos.

Order-Up is a group project that was constructed using MEAN stack. It was designed to allow users to log on and select menu items from local restaurants restaurant owners to go onto the site and create a profile for their establishment and menus.

Tea St. is a RoR web app that is designed as an online store that allows users to purchase their favorite teas
